TAG — Tight Aggressive
Plays few hands (VPIP <35%) but bets and raises aggressively (Agg >50%). Selective and disciplined — a strong, winning profile.
LAG — Loose Aggressive
Plays many hands (VPIP ≥35%) and plays them aggressively (Agg >50%). High variance, difficult to play against, can be very profitable.
LAP — Loose Passive
Plays many hands (VPIP ≥35%) but rarely bets or raises (Agg ≤50%). Calls too much — the classic "calling station."
Passive
Plays few hands (VPIP <35%) and doesn't apply pressure (Agg ≤50%). Tight but not aggressive — leaves value on the table.
